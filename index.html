Our Mission

Christ Care Ministry is a Christian organization dedicated to delivering food and essential items to food pantries and individuals in need. Our mission is to be a blessing to others through the resources and blessings we've been given.

Our Services

We provide essential items such as food, household items, and Christmas gifts to those in need. Our team travels across IL, OH, TN, KT, and more, reaching out to communities and spreading Christ's love and support.
